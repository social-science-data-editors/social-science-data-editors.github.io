Various useful pieces of information, such as terms of use for various data sources, repositories that may not be known as "trusted" in a formal sense, but which are robust enought to satisfy data editors’ needs, and other information. These are community-maintained by this group. If you have questions whether a dataset can be included in a replication package, or shared privately with the data editor, or neither of those options, you may find the answer here.
